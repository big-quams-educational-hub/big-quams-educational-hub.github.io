Cookie Policy

What cookies and similar technologies we use, and how you can manage them.

Last updated: 5 July 2026

1What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. We also use similar local storage technologies (like your browser's localStorage) for the same purposes described below. Big Quams Media® uses these to make the Platform work properly and to understand how it's used.

2Types of Cookies We Use

Essential / functional: remembers your dark/light mode preference and basic session information so the site works as expected as you navigate between pages.

Authentication: Firebase Authentication uses secure tokens to keep you signed in to your student dashboard between visits.

Analytics: Google Analytics cookies help us understand aggregated usage patterns (e.g. which pages are most visited) so we can improve the Platform. This data is not used to personally identify you for marketing purposes.

3Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies are set by services we rely on to run the Platform:

  • Google Analytics — usage analytics.
  • Google Firebase — authentication and app functionality.
  • Google Sign-In — if you choose to sign in with your Google account.

These providers have their own privacy and cookie practices, which we encourage you to review on their respective sites.

4Managing Cookies

Most browsers let you control or delete cookies through their settings. You can typically:

  • View what cookies are stored and delete them individually.
  • Block third-party cookies while allowing first-party cookies.
  • Block all cookies (note: this may prevent sign-in and some features from working correctly on Big Quams Media® and most other websites).

Because browser steps change over time, we recommend searching "manage cookies" plus the name of your browser (Chrome, Safari, Firefox, Edge) for current instructions.

5Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy as our tools and integrations evolve. Check back here for the latest version — the "Last updated" date above will always reflect the most recent revision.

Questions about our use of cookies? Reach us at bigquamsmedia@gmail.com or via WhatsApp below.
