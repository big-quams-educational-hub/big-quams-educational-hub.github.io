NELFUND Student Loan

Complete guide — eligibility, how to apply, repayment terms and the latest updates on the Nigerian federal student loan programme.

Government-backed fund
Who Qualifies
Eligibility Criteria
✅
Nigerian citizen enrolled in an accredited federal or state tertiary institution (university, polytechnic or college of education)
✅
Must have a valid National Identification Number (NIN) and Bank Verification Number (BVN)
✅
No history of default on any previous government loan programme
✅
Must provide a guarantor — a civil servant on Grade Level 12 or above, or a licensed professional (lawyer, doctor, engineer, etc.)
✅
Must have a valid school admission letter and current student ID
❌
Private institution students are currently NOT covered — eligibility may expand in future phases
Application Process
How to Apply
1
Register on NELFUND Portal
Visit nelfund.gov.ng and click "Apply Now". Create an account using your NIN and BVN.
2
Upload Your Documents
Upload your admission letter, school ID, guarantor form, passport photograph and bank details.
3
Submit Application
Review all details carefully and submit your application. You will receive a confirmation email and reference number.
4
Await Review
NELFUND reviews your application. This typically takes 2–4 weeks. Check your application status on the portal.
5
Tuition Disbursement
Once approved, your tuition funds are disbursed directly to your institution. Living allowance goes to your bank account.
6
Repayment Begins Later
Repayment starts 2 years after graduation or when employed — whichever comes first. Only 10% of monthly salary is deducted.
