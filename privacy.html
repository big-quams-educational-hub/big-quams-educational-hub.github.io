1Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Big Quams Media® ("we", "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our website and tools. We built Big Quams Media® to simplify campus life for Nigerian students, and we treat your data with the same care we'd want for our own.

2Information We Collect

Account information: when you create an account (email/password or Google Sign-In), we collect your name, email address, gender (optional, used for personalised guidance), and a profile photo if you sign in with Google.

Usage data: CBT practice sessions, scores, subjects practised, daily streaks, coin balances, and referral activity, so we can show you your own progress.

Content you submit: community posts, comments, student spotlight submissions, and messages sent through our contact/support channels.

Newsletter sign-ups: if you subscribe for updates, we store the email address you provide.

Technical data: basic analytics (via Google Analytics) such as pages visited and general device/browser information, used to understand how the Platform is used.

We do not knowingly collect sensitive information such as national ID numbers, financial account details, or biometric data.

3How We Use Your Information

  • To create and maintain your account and student dashboard.
  • To track your CBT performance, streaks, coins and referrals across the Platform.
  • To send you scholarship alerts, JAMB/admission updates, and campus news you've subscribed to.
  • To respond to support requests sent via WhatsApp or email.
  • To improve the Platform based on aggregated usage patterns.
  • To detect and prevent fraud, such as fake referral accounts.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

4How We Share Information

We use the following third-party service providers to operate the Platform:

  • Google Firebase (authentication, database/Firestore hosting) — stores your account and usage data securely.
  • Google Analytics — helps us understand aggregated site usage.
  • WhatsApp — if you choose to contact us or join our community group, standard WhatsApp terms apply to that conversation.

We may disclose information if required by law, to protect the rights and safety of Big Quams Media®, our users, or the public, or in connection with a future merger, acquisition, or sale of assets — in which case we will notify affected users.

5Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on Google Firebase infrastructure, which applies industry-standard security measures. We use reasonable technical and organisational safeguards to protect your information, but no online service can guarantee absolute security.

Please use a strong, unique password for your account and avoid sharing your login details with anyone.

6Cookies & Similar Technologies

We use cookies and similar local storage technologies for essential site functionality (such as remembering your dark/light mode preference) and for analytics. See our Cookie Policy for full details and how to control cookies in your browser.

7Your Rights & Choices

You can:

  • Access and update your profile information from your student dashboard.
  • Request a copy of your data or ask us to delete your account by emailing bigquamsmedia@gmail.com.
  • Unsubscribe from newsletter emails at any time using the link in those emails, or by contacting us.
  • Reset your password at any time using the "Forgot password?" option on the sign-in screen.

We will respond to verified requests within a reasonable timeframe.

8Children's Privacy

Big Quams Media® is intended for students preparing for or attending tertiary education, and is generally used by teenagers and young adults. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without appropriate parental involvement. If you believe a child under 13 has created an account without appropriate consent, please contact us so we can review and, if necessary, remove the account.

9Institutional & Partner Programs (Coming Soon)

Not yet live. As we introduce Institutional Partnerships, Campus Publisher, and Campus Ambassador programs, some limited additional information (such as institutional affiliation, or content-contribution history) may be collected specifically for participants in those programs.

No such data collection is active today. Before any of these programs launch, this Privacy Policy will be updated to describe exactly what additional data (if any) is collected, why, and how participants can control it.

10Data Retention

We retain your account information for as long as your account is active. If you request deletion, we will remove or anonymise your personal information within a reasonable period, except where we are required to retain certain records for legal or fraud-prevention purposes.

11Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as our Platform evolves. We will revise the "Last updated" date above and, for significant changes, make reasonable efforts to notify registered users.

12Contact Us

For any privacy questions or requests, reach us at bigquamsmedia@gmail.com or via WhatsApp below.
