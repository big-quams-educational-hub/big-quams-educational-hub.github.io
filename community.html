House Rules

Community Guidelines

1
Be respectful. Treat every member with dignity. Harassment, insults, or discrimination of any kind will result in immediate removal.
2
No spam or adverts. Do not post unsolicited advertisements, chain messages, or irrelevant content. Violators are removed without warning.
3
Share verified information only. Do not spread unconfirmed JAMB news, fake scholarship links, or misleading admission information.
4
Stay on topic. Keep discussions relevant to education, JAMB, admissions, scholarships, and campus life.
5
No adult or offensive content. This is a student platform. Explicit, violent, or politically inflammatory content is strictly prohibited.
6
Help each other. If you know the answer to a question, share it. The community grows when everyone contributes positively.
