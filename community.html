Community Guidelines
Community Rules
✅
Be respectful. Treat all members with dignity regardless of school, state or background. Zero tolerance for insults or discrimination.
✅
Share only verified information. Do not spread unconfirmed JAMB results, fake scholarship deadlines or rumours about admission.
✅
No spamming or advertising. Do not post unsolicited promotional messages, referral links or adverts without permission from BQM admin.
✅
No fraud or scams. Do not sell fake admission letters, results or documents. Any attempt will result in immediate removal and reporting.
✅
Stay on topic. This community is for education, admissions, scholarships and student life. Keep discussions relevant.
✅
Help each other. If you know the answer, share it. If you found a useful resource, share it. That is the spirit of Brainy Buddies.
