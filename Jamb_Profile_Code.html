🔑 JAMB Registration Help

Get Your JAMB Profile Code

Generate or retrieve your JAMB profile code via SMS — the first step to UTME and Direct Entry registration. Fast, simple and stress-free.

📱 Generate Your Profile Code
Enter your 11-digit NIN and tap Proceed. Your SMS app will open with the pre-filled message — just hit Send.
⚠️A small SMS charge (~₦50) applies. Ensure you have airtime and use the SIM linked to your NIN.
Step by Step

How It Works

1
Enter your NIN
Type your 11-digit National Identification Number in the box above. Make sure it's correct.
2
Select SMS gateway
Choose 55019 (default) or 66019 as your SMS destination number.
3
Tap Proceed
Your SMS app opens with the message pre-filled: NIN [your number]
4
Send the SMS
Hit Send from the SIM card linked to your NIN. Ensure you have at least ₦50 airtime.
5
Receive your code
Your 10-digit JAMB profile code arrives via SMS within minutes. Save it — you'll need it to register.
💬
Don't have access to your Gmail or NIN-linked SIM? Contact BQM support on WhatsApp →
For DE & Transfer Students

JAMB Regularization

JAMB Regularization makes a student's admission officially recognized by JAMB when it was granted outside the normal UTME or Direct Entry channels.

👤 Who Needs Regularization?
•Part-Time, NCE, OND and HND students
•Students admitted without JAMB registration
•Direct Entry candidates not on the JAMB matriculation list
🚫Warning: Without JAMB regularization, Direct Entry admission, university transfer or NYSC mobilization may be denied.
