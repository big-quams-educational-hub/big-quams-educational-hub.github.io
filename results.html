Frequently Asked

Common Questions

My WAEC result is showing "Result Withheld" — what does it mean?
A withheld result usually means WAEC has flagged your paper for malpractice investigation. You need to visit the WAEC office in your state with your exam slip and a guardian. Do not panic — many cases are resolved after verification.
I don't have a scratch card. Can I still check my WAEC/NECO result?
No — WAEC and NECO require a scratch card PIN to view results. JAMB is the exception: UTME results are free. Buy your scratch card from any accredited agent or bank. Avoid unknown online sellers.
How do I check my JAMB admission status on CAPS?
Go to jamb.gov.ng → click eFacility → log in with the email you used during JAMB registration → click "Check Admission Status". If offered, you'll see your school and course, and you can then "Accept" the offer.
My NECO result shows some subjects as "Awaiting" — is that normal?
Yes, it's normal. "Awaiting" means those subject results have not been fully processed yet. Check back after 1–2 weeks. If it persists beyond 4 weeks, visit your school or the NECO state office.
I forgot the email I used to register for JAMB. What do I do?
Visit the nearest JAMB office with a valid ID and your JAMB profile code (printed on your exam slip). They can help you recover or reset access to your account.
Can I check results on my phone?
Yes! All result portals work on mobile browsers. Use Chrome or Firefox for best results. Make sure you have a stable internet connection. If a page doesn't load, try again in a few minutes.
